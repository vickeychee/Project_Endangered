Ivory Tusks
Asian elephants use their ivory tusks for many purposes. This includes digging, pulling trees out of the ground, and displaying.
Human Settlements
Human settlements, including houses and apartments, have impacted the grasslands environment where elephants live. As a result, about 70% of Asian elephants are now living outside of protected areas.
