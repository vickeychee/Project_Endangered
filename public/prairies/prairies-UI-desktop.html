Water the Crops
Ponds and marshes are an important part of the whooping crane’s habitat. As humans build more buildings and farms these marshes become drained. Water the yellow wheat plants on the farm to see how it effects the environment!
Instructions

Click on the pond to fill the bucket with water.
Find the yellow wheat plants and click on them to water them.

Oh No!
You weren’t able to water all of the crops because the pond ran out of water! Did you notice how the pond became more drained every time? This is what happens in the wet prairies as more buildings and farms are built there. This destroys the whooping cranes home, leaving them with no where to live. If whooping cranes do not have a home, they will eventually become extinct.
